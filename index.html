01
Clinical Workflow Design

Redesigning care delivery workflows for frontline teams

Amae Health · 2023–Present

Frontline Member Care Advocates were navigating a fragmented suite of internal tools, creating friction at every handoff. Tasks that required crossing between systems led to errors, delays, and advocate burnout. I was brought in to own the end-to-end experience — from defining the problem space to shipping modular components that could be deployed without engineering involvement.

Working directly with Clinical Operations, Data Science, and Product leadership, I led service design workshops and workflow mapping sessions to diagnose the root causes. I defined KPIs and OKRs in partnership with Data Science, designed a modular component framework that enabled clinical leadership to rapidly test new protocols, and influenced the 6–12 month product roadmap.

26% Workflow efficiency improvement
0 Engineering resources needed to deploy new protocols
6–12mo Roadmap influence across clinical tooling

02
Internal Tools · Dashboard Design

Manager-facing dashboards for operational visibility

Teladoc Health · 2021–2023

Care operations managers at Teladoc had no centralized view of team performance. Queue health, case volume, resolution trends, and workflow bottlenecks lived in disconnected reports and spreadsheets. Decisions were reactive, not informed.

I led the design of a manager-facing dashboard system from discovery through launch — running stakeholder interviews, workflow mapping, and usability testing with operations managers across time zones. The system gave managers real-time visibility into what was working, what wasn't, and where to intervene.

21% Reduction in case resolution time
1 Unified view replacing fragmented reports

03
Brand & Presentation Systems

Scalable presentation system for multi-team storytelling

Teladoc Health · 2018–2021

Marketing, Clinical Leadership, Product Marketing, and internal communications teams at Teladoc each maintained their own slide decks — inconsistently branded, hard to update, and rarely at the quality level needed for executive or external audiences.

I designed a modular, scalable PowerPoint and Google Slides system adopted across the organization. The work included executive presentation design for leadership reviews and care delivery updates, a reusable infographic and diagram library, and visual systems for translating complex clinical concepts into accessible narratives.

About

The designer behind the work

I've spent a decade at the intersection of healthcare complexity and human-centered design — working at organizations where the stakes are real and the systems are never simple.

My background in Public Health Policy gives me a foundation most product designers don't have: I understand how care is structured, where regulatory constraints live, and why clinical teams behave the way they do. That context makes me a stronger design partner across clinical, product, and executive stakeholders.

I design for the people who run care — the advocates managing queues, the managers reading dashboards at 7am, the clinical leaders deploying new protocols. I've led workshops, mapped service blueprints, defined KPIs, and shipped modular systems. I care as much about the outcome as the artifact.
